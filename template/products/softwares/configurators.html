Configurators

Customization, blending and design

07

Configurators 3D is a real time 3D application that embeds new generation gaming and design software, displaying products in hight quality 3D visuals and with outstanding fidelity.

Customers can choose, redesign, add and mix multiple pieces and features, so that they can achieve their unique ideal item, the "one".

Advanced technology, smart algorithms and a user-friendly interface make configurators 3D a tool that engages everyone's vision in a creative exercise, with the added benefits of on-spot price calculation and data integration with existing production and logistic programs.

Modules

01

3D Customizer Module

3D configuration and products creation
Users can modify, mix and interchange multiple predefined components. Optional algorithms and parameters ensure rules related with parts positioning and association, restrictions, area changes, new line generation, subtle and delicate changes etc

02

Price Module

Instant price calculator
Every change applied to the product during the design process is instantly reflected in the price calculation, enabling live, on the spot and as needed cost adjustments.

03

Production Module

Data export, ERP/ MRP/ CRM integrator

Interchangeable Configurator

Software solutions for easy choosing, pleasant blending and sophisticated design

The Interchangeable configurator creates a wide range of options and structures, relying on the 3D Mesh technology, intertwining concept and multiple predefined parameters.

Customers are able to choose and combine all these options in (re)designing their own solitaire rings, eternity rings or the version that intertwins these two.

Patterns are available as a base for each product and customers are able to personalize them by changing the material, shape, structure, stone (number, colour, size, placement).

Vehicles 3D Configurator

Disruptive technology for shorter routes towards customers' needs and 'wheels'

Configurator 3D provides a showroom setting, in which the customers can configure cars, boats, planes and many other vehicle type. The application offers a broad range of adaptable features, such as dimension, colour, texture and extra accessories.
High level technology and qualitative animations contribute to a realistic feeling, customers being able to observe general or technical details. The application can simulate how the rims cover rotates, how the doors open and close, how the steering wheels moves or how headlights are displayed (using various methods, be it halogen, xenon LED or laser lights).
The data export module ensures that every product change and selection is registered and shared with the production department and other relevant internal platforms(ERP, MRP, CRM).

Safes Configurator

Configurator 3D combines 3D Mesh technology, multiple predefined parameters and advanced concepts to provide a reliable solution in safes design and setup.
The interface becomes a trustworthy guide through the technical flow steps, body generation and elements creation (door, handle, locker, drawers).
