Hidraulica

Project example

A project that describes a system of two hydraulic torque wrenches, its components and their interactions, as an interactive simulation of a hydraulic torque wrench and its functionalities, separately and as part of a system.
Every technical component (reaction arm or boot, drive retainer, reaction pawl lever etc) is separeted and 3D animated, showing in an easy to understand manner what happens during bolting/unbolting time, how the torque is generated by hydraulic means or if it is distributed everywhere, how a bolt gets tightended/untightened to a specified torque, twisting or rotating forces and their direction.
